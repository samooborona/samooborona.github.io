What is the Kadochnikov System?

The Kadochnikov System is a unique author's system of martial art, developed by Alexey Alexeevich Kadochnikov and further developed under the guidance of his son, Arkady Alexeevich.

It is not a set of techniques, but a holistic philosophy of movement and a life support system based on the laws of biomechanics, physics, geometry, psychophysiology and other sciences.

The system includes the following sections:

  • Low acrobatics and rational movement: the basics of survival and mobility.
  • Applied hand-to-hand combat: efficiency as a priority.
  • Psychobiomechanics and stress resistance: self-control in an emotionally unstable stressful environment.

Training Program

TOPIC 1

Body preparation. Work on the floor plane. Stance and movements. Special preparatory exercises of low acrobatics: somersaults, falls, rolls. Removing fear and tension. Formation of basic motor skills - the foundation of the entire System.

TOPIC 2

Unbalancing. Contact with the opponent. Contact zones. Impact without brute physical force. How to deprive an opponent of stability with optimal efforts.

TOPIC 3

Release from grabs. Countering physical impact in grabs. Basic skills that allow you to use the opponent's force against him.

TOPIC 4

Protection against strikes. Principles of building protection against strikes. Using theoretical forms of physics, mechanics, geometry to meet the strike. Consideration of psychophysical processes.
